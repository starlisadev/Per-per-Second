Got it. You're 100% correct. The theme *is* the visual language.

As a senior dev, I can tell you that for a project like this, your UI/UX has to be better than just "looking good." It needs to solve a specific problem: **building trust for a new type of financial transaction.**

The user is streaming their money. Your UI must make them feel secure, in control, and informed.

The best visual theme is a **"Modern Tech / FinTech" Dark Mode**.

Think of the UI from **Stripe**, **Coinbase**, or a modern **Plex** media server. It should be clean, spacious, data-rich, and feel precise.

Here is a breakdown of that visual theme.



### 1. Color Palette: "Trustworthy Dark Mode"

A dark theme is perfect for a streaming service because it makes the video content "pop" and feels premium.

* **Backgrounds:** Don't use pure black (`#000000`). Use a deep, dark blue or charcoal. This feels more professional and less "OLED-burn-in."
    * *Example:* A "slate" or "navy" color (`#0F172A` or `#111827`).
* **Card/Panel Backgrounds:** A slightly lighter shade of your background.
    * *Example:* A "dark grey" (`#1E293B`).
* **Primary Accent Color:** This is your *most important* color. It's for buttons, links, and all financial "actions." You need one, high-contrast, "tech" color.
    * *Example:* A vibrant **"Stellar Blue"** (`#00A9FF`) or a **"Tech Green"** (`#10B981`).
* **Text:**
    * *Headings:* Pure white (`#FFFFFF`) or a very light grey (`#F1F5F9`).
    * *Body/Descriptions:* A softer, "off-white" grey (`#E2E8F0`). This is easier on the eyes.

### 2. Typography: "Clarity & Precision"

This is a place where you can really impress. You need two font styles.

1.  **For UI & Headings (The "Human" Font):** Use a clean, modern, sans-serif font. This makes the UI feel friendly and easy to read.
    * **Recommendation:** `Inter`. It's free, professional, and designed specifically for user interfaces. `Poppins` or `Manrope` are also great choices.

2.  **For Numbers (The "Data" Font):** This is the pro-tip. For any number that represents money, time, or an ID, use a **monospace** font.
    * **Recommendation:** `Roboto Mono` or `Fira Code`.
    * **Why?** It visually separates "data" from "text." It makes your balances and transaction logs look like a precise, technical "bank statement," which builds subconscious trust.

**Example:**
* **Title:** `Sintel` (in `Inter` font)
* **Balance:** `4.97000 XLM` (in `Roboto Mono` font)

### 3. Layout: "Spacious & Familiar"

* **Spacing:** Use generous padding and "white space" (or "dark space" in this case). A crowded UI feels cheap and chaotic. A spacious UI feels calm and premium. Use a standard 8-point grid system (e.g., margins and padding in multiples of 8px).
* **Layout:** Use **Netflix-style carousels** for the browse page. This is what users know. Don't reinvent the wheel.
* **Corners & Borders:** Use subtle rounded corners (`rounded-lg`) on all your cards and buttons. Use *very* subtle borders (`border-gray-700`) to separate panels.
